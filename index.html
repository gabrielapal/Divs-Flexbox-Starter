I'm a Niagara Falls, Ontario native and first-generation Canadian. I have roots in Mexico and Peru and have family spread out in different parts of the world. Perhaps its why I've always loved traveling and exploring different places and cultures!

A big passion of mine is creating art. From as long as I can remember, I've always been drawing, painting or engulfed with a need to transform a blank page into something wonderful. I graduated from McMaster University with a degree in Fine Art, where I was challenged and encouraged to bring complex concepts to life through mediums like painting, print-making, paper-making and sculpture.

A fun fact about me: I love to bake and experiment with new recipes in the kitchen- there's something so relaxing and therapeutic about how methodical and precise the whole process is!
